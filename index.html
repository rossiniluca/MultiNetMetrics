MultiNetMetrics   H2020-MSCA-IF-2018. PROJECT REFERENCE: 796902

The aims of MultiNetMetrics (Multiplex Network Econometrics) are to study

  1. the dynamic behaviour of networks;
  2. its possible dynamic linkages between different network layers.
It aims to develop novel and appropriate econometric methods to address the two research challenges in a computationally feasible way.
The project is hosted by VU Amsterdam.
